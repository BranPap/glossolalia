Glossolalia

A free, weird little language tool for language nerds

Glossolalia is a free, open-source language exercise app built with jsPsych. No frills, no fees, no ads; just the most barebones language practice you can imagine. Each exercise simply drills the relevant forms.

This is a labor of love. I will aim to grow this slowly, but please do feel free to reach out to me if you find any issues in the meantime.
