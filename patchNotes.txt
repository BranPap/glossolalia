## 7th March 2025: v0.1 | Initial Release 
(or at least the beginning of better documentation)

- Current "support" for 3 languages
    - Finnish (82 Verbs: 82 Present Indicative, 27 Past Indicative)
    - Spanish (10 Verbs: 10 Present Indicative, 1 Future Indicative, 1 Past Indicative)
    - Lithuanian (1 Verb: 1 Present Indicative)
- 3 Modes
    - Conjugation mode (type the verb form given the pronoun; translation is available by hovering/clicking the verb)
    - Translation mode (translate the verb from the target language into English. Reverse direction toggles translating from English into the target language)
    - Training mode (match the verb forms to the right pronouns)
- Themes!
    - Standard
    - Witchy
    - Forest
    - Aquatic
    - Space
    - Horror
